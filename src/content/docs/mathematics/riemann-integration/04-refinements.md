$Q$ is called a refinement of $P \iff $ $P$ and $Q$ are partitions of $[a,b]$
**and** $P\subseteq Q$.

In that case:

```math
L(f;P)
\le
L(f;Q)
\le
U(f;Q)
\le
U(f;P)
```

If $P_1$ and $P_2$ are partitions of $[a,b]$, then $Q=P_1\cup P_2$ is a
refinement of both $P_1$ and $P_2$.
