Suppose $f,g$ are continuous functions on $[a,b]$ and $g \ge 0$. Then
$\exists x \in (a,b)$:

```math
f(x)
\int_a^b g
=
\int_a^b fg
```

:::note[Proof Hint]

Consider this and proof is similar to IVT.

```math
mg \le fg \le Mg
```

:::
